Further details and background

DIT ‘Meet the buyer’ events exist to connect UK products and services with interested buyers from overseas. If your company exports - or if you’re interested in beginning to sell to new global markets - then ‘Meet the buyer’ events offer a great opportunity to meet face to face with potential suitors for your products and services. And if you’re a non UK based company interested in buying a particular product or service, ‘Meet the buyer’ events allow you to explore the British market for potential suppliers that can help with your requirements. How it works The concept is simple. DIT sources a selection of buyers to participate in a ‘Meet the buyer’ event. Once registered to attend, UK businesses can then request face to face meeting slots with any of these buyers over the course of the event. Buyers receive attendee meeting requests - and if they wish to meet, a meeting will be scheduled. On the day of the event, UK sellers meet up with international buyers. Both parties hopefully leave the event with a host of new contacts, promising leads - and ultimately, fresh business.
