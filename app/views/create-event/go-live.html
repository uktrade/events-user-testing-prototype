How does this work?

You can use tracking links to identify where webpage traffic and event registrations are coming from.

This allows you to find out exactly which promotion channels are creating the most amount of webpage traffic and converting most effectively into event registrations. For example, you can measure the precise number of registrations that originate from Twitter, by using that channel's unique link in your tweets.

Traffic and registrations originating from any of these links will appear in your performance dashboard.
